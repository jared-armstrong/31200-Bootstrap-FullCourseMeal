Jared's Full Course Bootstrap Meal
Caesar Salad

Cut up lettuce, shred cheese of your choice, and provide some crutons for some additional crunch and flavor!

Chicken Soup

Grab chicken, carrots, celery and onion. Boil water and place all ingredients in a pot. Make sure to cook the chicken and then shred it once it is ready. Filter everything out and place back in when ready. When the soup looks done, add seasonings of your choice!

French Fries

Grab a couple fresh potatoes, slice them up to your liking and then place them in the air fryer at 375 degrees Fahreneheit until they are nice and crispy. You can add any seasonings you wish!

Cheesburger

Unthaw ground beef or if you are vegan, unthaw a vegan patty. Make sure your pan is heated sufficiently and then cook the burger to your liking. Make sure you provide at least some salt and pepper seasoning for the burger and cook it to your liking. Add on some tomatoes, onions, and lettuce for more flavor.

Fudge Brownie

Following the instructions on Betty Crocker's brownie mix, make your brownies and put them into the oven until they are ready. Make sure you have plenty of eggs, milk, fudge topping and maybe some ice cream to go on top!
